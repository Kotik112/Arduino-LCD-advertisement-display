


Welcome to our billboard! 


To run the show please compile and enter serial portnumbers to be connected to in this manner - COM3, COM4.....

When you are succesfully connected please choose your choice of action in the menu shown in the program. 

Please note that you can ad a maximum of five companies and their slogans to be displayed. 


Important Note (!!) 

This is how you compile :

        g++ -Wall  main.cpp ad_manager.cpp company.cpp menu.cpp serial.c -o program.exe

Or....
If you have make, just write :

        "make"
